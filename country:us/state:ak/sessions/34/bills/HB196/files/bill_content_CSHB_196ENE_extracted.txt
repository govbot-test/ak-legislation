AS 42.45.045(a) is amended to read: 04 (a) A renewable energy grant fund is established as a separate fund to finance 05 certain energy projects in Alaska for the purpose of providing affordable energy to 06 all communities in the state. 07 * Sec. 2. AS 42.45.045(d) is amended to read: 08 (d) The authority shall, in consultation with the advisory committee 09 established under (i) of this section and the Department of Natural Resources, 10 (1) develop a methodology for determining the order of projects that 11 may receive assistance, including separate requirements for grant eligibility, and adopt 12 regulations identifying criteria to evaluate the benefit and feasibility of projects for 13 which an applicant applies for support from the legislature, with the most weight being 14 given to projects that serve any area in which the average cost of energy to each 15 resident of the area exceeds the average cost to each resident of other areas of the 01 state, and significant weight being given to a statewide balance of grant funds and to 02 the amount of matching funds an applicant is able to make available; 03 (2) provide technical assistance to communities with a population 04 of less than 2,000 to help the community prepare and submit grant applications 05 for projects; 06 (3) annually solicit and review grant applications for projects; 07 (4) make recommendations to the legislature for renewable power 08 production reimbursement grants; and 09 (5) [(3)] not later than 10 days after the first day of each regular 10 legislative session, submit to the legislature a report summarizing and reviewing each 11 grant application submitted under this section and a recommended priority for 12 awarding grants. 13 * Sec. 3. AS 42.45.045 is amended by adding a new subsection to read: 14 (m) The advisory committee appointed under (i) of this section shall elect a 15 chair from among its members. The chair serves for a term of two years. The chair 16 may not serve for consecutive terms. After a year of not serving as chair, the member 17 is eligible for election as chair again. The advisory committee shall meet at the call of 18 the chair.

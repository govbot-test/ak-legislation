establishing deadlines for the payment of grants, contracts, and reimbursement agreements to nonprofit organizations, municipalities, and Alaska Native organizations; relating to reports from state agencies to the legislature detailing late payments made during 2026; relating to the state financial transactions Internet website; and providing for an effective date." 00 SENATE CS FOR CS FOR HOUSE BILL NO. 133(FIN) 01 "An Act establishing deadlines for the payment of contracts under the State 02 Procurement Code; establishing deadlines for the payment of grants, contracts, and 03 reimbursement agreements to nonprofit organizations, municipalities, and Alaska 04 Native organizations; relating to reports from state agencies to the legislature detailing 05 late payments made during 2026; relating to the state financial transactions Internet 06 website; and providing for an effective date." 07 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF ALASKA: 08 * Section 1. AS 36.30 is amended by adding a new section to read: 09 Sec. 36.30.970. Payment deadline and interest. (a) An agency shall pay a 10 contractor for satisfactory performance on a contract within 45 calendar days after the 11 agency receives a payment request from the contractor that complies with the contract. 12 (b) If a contractor is not paid as required by (a) of this section, the agency 13 shall pay interest on the unpaid amount of the required payment from the 46th 01 calendar day after the date the agency receives a payment request under (a) of this 02 section at an interest rate that is equal to the rate set out in AS 45.45.010(a). 03 (c) If an agency withholds payment because of unsatisfactory performance or 04 if the payment request made under (a) of this section does not comply with the 05 requirements of the contract, within eight working days after receipt of the payment 06 request the agency shall notify the contractor in writing stating specifically why 07 payment is being withheld and what remedial actions may be taken by the contractor 08 to receive the full payment. 09 (d) If the notification by the agency required by (c) of this section does not 10 comply with (c) of this section, the agency shall pay interest on the withheld amount 11 from the eighth working day after receipt of the initial payment request until the 12 agency provides notice that does comply with (c) of this section. 13 (e) If payment is withheld under (c) of this section, the agency shall pay the 14 withheld amount within 45 calendar days after the contractor satisfactorily completes 15 the remedial actions identified in the notice. If the withheld amount is not paid within 16 the 45 calendar days, the agency shall pay interest on the withheld amount from the 17 45th calendar day at an interest rate that is equal to the rate set out in AS 45.45.010(a). 18 (f) The obligation to pay interest under this section does not apply to 19 retainage. 20 (g) This section does not apply to 21 (1) a public construction or public works contract subject to 22 AS 36.90.200; 23 (2) an agreement subject to AS 36.90.310; or 24 (3) the division in the Department of Military and Veterans' Affairs 25 that is responsible for homeland security and emergency management; however, the 26 division shall make reasonable efforts to comply with the deadlines established under 27 this section. 28 (h) In this section, "contractor" means a person who has been awarded a 29 contract with an agency. 30 * Sec. 2. AS 36.30.970(a), enacted by sec. 1 of this Act, is amended to read: 31 (a) An agency shall pay a contractor for satisfactory performance on a contract 01 within 30 [45] calendar days after the agency receives a payment request from the 02 contractor that complies with the contract. 03 * Sec. 3. AS 36.30.970(b), enacted by sec. 1 of this Act, is amended to read: 04 (b) If a contractor is not paid as required by (a) of this section, the agency 05 shall pay interest on the unpaid amount of the required payment from the 31st [46th] 06 calendar day after the date the agency receives a payment request under (a) of this 07 section at an interest rate that is equal to the rate set out in AS 45.45.010(a). 08 * Sec. 4. AS 36.30.970(e), enacted by sec. 1 of this Act, is amended to read: 09 (e) If payment is withheld under (c) of this section, the agency shall pay the 10 withheld amount within 30 [45] calendar days after the contractor satisfactorily 11 completes the remedial actions identified in the notice. If the withheld amount is not 12 paid within the 30 [45] calendar days, the agency shall pay interest on the withheld 13 amount from the 30th [45th] calendar day at an interest rate that is equal to the rate set 14 out in AS 45.45.010(a). 15 * Sec. 5. AS 36.90 is amended by adding a new section to article 4 to read: 16 Sec. 36.90.310. Payment deadline and interest for grants and 17 reimbursement agreements to nonprofit organizations, municipalities, and 18 Alaska Native organizations. (a) An agency shall pay a nonprofit organization, 19 municipality, or Alaska Native organization for satisfactory performance of a grant or 20 reimbursement agreement within 45 calendar days after the agency receives a payment 21 request from the nonprofit organization, municipality, or Alaska Native organization 22 that complies with the grant or reimbursement agreement. If the agency uses federal 23 funds to pay the grant or reimbursement agreement, the agency shall pay for 24 satisfactory performance within 45 calendar days after the agency receives a payment 25 request that complies with the grant or reimbursement agreement, or within 45 26 calendar days after the agency receives the federal funds, whichever is later. 27 (b) If a nonprofit organization, municipality, or Alaska Native organization is 28 not paid as required by (a) of this section, the agency shall pay interest on the unpaid 29 amount of the required payment at an interest rate that is equal to the rate set out in 30 AS 45.45.010(a) from 31 (1) the 45th calendar day after the date the agency receives a payment 01 request under (a) of this section if the agency uses federal funds to make the payment; 02 (2) the 45th calendar day after the date the agency receives a payment 03 request under (a) of this section for all other payments for grants and reimbursement 04 agreements. 05 (c) If an agency withholds payment because of unsatisfactory performance or 06 if the payment request made under (a) of this section does not comply with the 07 requirements of the grant or reimbursement agreement, within eight working days 08 after receipt of the payment request the agency shall notify the nonprofit organization, 09 municipality, or Alaska Native organization in writing stating specifically why the 10 payment is being withheld and what remedial actions may be taken by the nonprofit 11 organization, municipality, or Alaska Native organization to receive the full payment. 12 (d) If the notification by the agency required by (c) of this section does not 13 comply with (c) of this section, the agency shall pay interest on the withheld amount 14 from the eighth working day after receipt of the initial payment request until the 15 agency provides notice that does comply with (c) of this section. 16 (e) If payment is withheld under (c) of this section, the agency shall pay the 17 withheld amount within 45 calendar days after the nonprofit organization, 18 municipality, or Alaska Native organization satisfactorily completes the remedial 19 actions identified in the notice. If the withheld amount is not paid within the 45 20 calendar days, the agency shall pay interest on the withheld amount from the 45th 21 calendar day at an interest rate that is equal to the rate set out in AS 45.45.010(a). 22 (f) This section does not apply to the division in the Department of Military 23 and Veterans' Affairs that is responsible for homeland security and emergency 24 management. However, the division shall make reasonable efforts to comply with the 25 deadlines established under this section. 26 (g) In this section, 27 (1) "agency" has the meaning given in AS 36.30.990; 28 (2) "Alaska Native organization" has the meaning given in 29 AS 18.66.020(d); 30 (3) "grant" means a legal instrument of financial assistance between an 31 agency and a nonstate entity that is used to enter into a relationship for which the 01 principal purpose is to provide funds to the nonstate entity; "grant" does not include 02 (A) a cooperative agreement that provides for substantial 03 involvement between the agency and the recipient in carrying out the activity 04 contemplated by the award; 05 (B) a legal instrument used by an agency to acquire property or 06 services for the direct benefit of an agency; 07 (C) cash assistance to an individual; 08 (D) a subsidy; 09 (E) a loan; 10 (F) a loan guarantee; 11 (G) insurance; 12 (H) state funding that is required annually and is calculated 13 through a formula set in statute; 14 (I) a permanent fund dividend; or 15 (J) a grant appropriated to a municipality under AS 37.05.315 16 or a named recipient under AS 37.05.316; 17 (4) "nonprofit organization" has the meaning given in 18 AS 38.05.097(c). 19 * Sec. 6. AS 36.90.310(a), enacted by sec. 5 of this Act, is amended to read: 20 (a) An agency shall pay a nonprofit organization, municipality, or Alaska 21 Native organization for satisfactory performance of a grant or reimbursement 22 agreement within 30 [45] calendar days after the agency receives a payment request 23 from the nonprofit organization, municipality, or Alaska Native organization that 24 complies with the grant or reimbursement agreement. If the agency uses federal funds 25 to pay the grant or reimbursement agreement, the agency shall pay for satisfactory 26 performance within 30 [45] calendar days after the agency receives a payment request 27 that complies with the grant or reimbursement agreement, or within 30 [45] calendar 28 days after the agency receives the federal funds, whichever is later. 29 * Sec. 7. AS 36.90.310(b), enacted by sec. 5 of this Act, is amended to read: 30 (b) If a nonprofit organization, municipality, or Alaska Native organization is 31 not paid as required by (a) of this section, the agency shall pay interest on the unpaid 01 amount of the required payment at an interest rate that is equal to the rate set out in 02 AS 45.45.010(a) from 03 (1) the 30th [45th] calendar day after the date the agency receives a 04 payment request under (a) of this section if the agency uses federal funds to make the 05 payment; 06 (2) the 30th [45th] calendar day after the date the agency receives a 07 payment request under (a) of this section for all other payments for grants and 08 reimbursement agreements. 09 * Sec. 8. AS 36.90.310(e), enacted by sec. 5 of this Act, is amended to read: 10 (e) If payment is withheld under (c) of this section, the agency shall pay the 11 withheld amount within 30 [45] calendar days after the nonprofit organization, 12 municipality, or Alaska Native organization satisfactorily completes the remedial 13 actions identified in the notice. If the withheld amount is not paid within the 30 [45] 14 calendar days, the agency shall pay interest on the withheld amount from the 30th 15 [45th] calendar day at an interest rate that is equal to the rate set out in 16 AS 45.45.010(a). 17 * Sec. 9. AS 37.05.215(a) is amended to read: 18 (a) The Department of Administration shall develop, operate, and maintain a 19 searchable Internet website that is accessible to the public at no cost and that provides 20 financial information available from the central accounting system or the annual 21 financial report prepared and filed under AS 37.05.210(a)(1), describing 22 (1) unaudited state revenue for the preceding month, including 23 (A) a receipt or deposit by a state agency into a fund or account 24 established within the state treasury; 25 (B) proceeds from taxes received, categorized by source type, 26 including a compulsory contribution imposed by the state for the purpose of 27 financing services; 28 (C) state agency earnings, including amounts collected for sales 29 or services, licenses or permits issued, or otherwise received by a state agency 30 under the agency's regulations; 31 (D) revenue received for the use of state money or property, 01 including interest and lease payments; 02 (E) gifts, donations, and federal receipts; and 03 (F) other revenue; 04 (2) unaudited expenditures for the preceding month, including 05 (A) the name and location of any person to whom payment was 06 made; 07 (B) the amount of the expenditure disbursed; 08 (C) the type of transaction, by account code, including the 09 purpose of the expenditure; 10 (D) for each payment made to a person under a contract or 11 to a nonprofit organization, municipality, or Alaska Native organization 12 under a grant or reimbursement agreement, whether the payment was 13 made 30 or more days after the payment is due; 14 (E) other information specified by the Department of 15 Administration; 16 (3) the estimated balances, for the preceding month, of the statutory 17 budget reserve fund (AS 37.05.540), the constitutional budget reserve fund (art. IX, 18 sec. 17, Constitution of the State of Alaska), and the permanent fund earnings reserve 19 account (AS 37.13.145); 20 (4) the amount deposited into the permanent fund under art. IX, sec 15, 21 Constitution of the State of Alaska for the preceding fiscal year; 22 (5) state revenue and expenditures, summarized for the preceding 23 fiscal year, as follows: 24 (A) general fund revenue sources; 25 (B) general fund expenditures categorized by function; 26 (C) general fund expenditures categorized by state agency; 27 (D) general fund expenditures categorized by account; and 28 (E) total general fund revenue versus expenditures; 29 (6) total assets versus liabilities at the end of the fiscal year; [AND] 30 (7) for the preceding 10 years, by fiscal year, the following: 31 (A) the number of full-time, part-time, and temporary 01 employees employed by the state, by state agency; 02 (B) the number of independent contractors engaged by the 03 state, by state agency; 04 (C) the total long-term debt owed by the state, by state agency; 05 (D) total general fund expenditures; and 06 (E) total general fund payroll, by state agency; and 07 (8) payments owed to a person under a contract or to a nonprofit 08 organization, municipality, or Alaska Native organization under a grant or 09 reimbursement agreement that are 30 or more days past due. 10 * Sec. 10. AS 37.05.215(b) is amended to read: 11 (b) The Department of Administration shall update the information described 12 in (a)(1) - (3) and (8) of this section on the Internet website on a monthly basis and the 13 information described in (a)(4) - (7) of this section on an annual basis. To the extent 14 practicable, information on the website should be sorted by state agency, accounting 15 category, accounting code, recipient, purpose, amount, and date. The Department of 16 Administration shall maintain and store the information required by this section on the 17 website for at least 10 years. 18 * Sec. 11. The uncodified law of the State of Alaska is amended by adding a new section to 19 read: 20 REPORT TO THE LEGISLATURE. An agency shall provide a report to the 21 legislature by the first day of the First Regular Session of the Thirty-Fifth Alaska State 22 Legislature detailing the number of late payments the agency made during 2026 to a nonprofit 23 organization, municipality, or Alaska Native organization for satisfactory performance of a 24 grant or reimbursement agreement, or to a contractor for satisfactory performance on a 25 contract, the reason the payments were late, and the length of the delays. The agency shall 26 deliver the report to the senate secretary and the chief clerk of the house of representatives 27 and notify the legislature that the report is available. In this section, 28 (1) "agency" has the meaning given in AS 36.30.990; 29 (2) "contractor" means 30 (A) a person who has been awarded a contract with an agency; or 31 (B) a prime contractor as that term is defined in AS 36.90.290; 01 (3) "grant" has the meaning given in AS 36.90.310, enacted by sec. 5 of this 02 Act; 03 (4) "nonprofit organization" has the meaning given in AS 38.05.097(c). 04 * Sec. 12. Section 11 of this Act takes effect immediately under AS 01.10.070(c). 05 * Sec. 13. Sections 1 and 5 of this Act take effect July 1, 2027. 06 * Sec. 14. Sections 2 - 4 and 6 - 8 of this Act take effect July 1, 2028.

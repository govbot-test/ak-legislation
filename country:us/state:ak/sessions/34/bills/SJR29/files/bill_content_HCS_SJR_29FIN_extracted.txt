Article IX, Constitution of the State of Alaska, is amended by adding a new 05 section to read: 06 Section 18. Public Education Fund. (a) There is established as a separate 07 fund in the State treasury the public education fund. The fund consists of land 08 transferred and money appropriated to the fund by the legislature, the proceeds of a 09 State tax or license dedicated to and deposited into the fund by law, and all income 10 earned on the fund. Money in the public education fund shall be invested so as to yield 11 competitive market rates to the fund. Section 7 of this article does not apply to land 12 transferred or money appropriated to the fund, the proceeds of a State tax or license 13 dedicated to and deposited into the fund by law, or income earned on the fund. 14 (b) Money in the public education fund may be appropriated only for public 15 education, as defined by law. 16 * Sec. 2. The amendment proposed by this resolution shall be placed before the voters of the 01 state at the next general election in conformity with art. XIII, sec. 1, Constitution of the State 02 of Alaska, and the election laws of the state.

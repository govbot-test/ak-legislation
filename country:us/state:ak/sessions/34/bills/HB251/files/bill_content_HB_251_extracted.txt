AS 01.10.060(a)(8) is amended to read: 05 (8) "person" includes a corporation, company, partnership, firm, 06 association, organization, business trust, or society, as well as a natural person; or a 07 human entity that has the moral right of self-determination; "person" does not 08 include an environmental element, artificial intelligence, animal, or inanimate 09 object; 10 * Sec. 2. AS 11.81.900(b)(48) is amended to read: 11 (48) "person" means a natural person or a human entity that has the 12 moral right of self-determination and, when appropriate, an organization, 13 government, or governmental instrumentality; "person" does not include an 14 environmental element, artificial intelligence, animal, or inanimate object; 01 * Sec. 3. AS 11.81.900(b) is amended by adding a new paragraph to read: 02 (71) "life" means the property or quality that distinguishes a living 03 human organism from a dead organism or inanimate matter and that is manifested in 04 the function of metabolism, growth, or reproduction, a response to stimuli, or 05 adaptation to the environment, each of which originates within the organism. 06 * Sec. 4. The uncodified law of the State of Alaska is amended by adding a new section to 07 read: 08 APPLICABILITY. AS 11.81.900(b)(48), as amended by sec. 2 of this Act, and AS 09 11.81.900(b)(71), enacted by sec. 3 of this Act, apply to offenses committed on or after the 10 effective date of this Act. 11 * Sec. 5.

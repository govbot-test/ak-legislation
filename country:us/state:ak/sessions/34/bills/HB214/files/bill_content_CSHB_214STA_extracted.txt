AS 28.20.330(b) is amended to read: 05 (b) The proof required by (a) of this section shall be maintained 06 (1) for one year from the date the judgment is stayed or satisfied if 07 the suspension is the person's first suspension under AS 28.20.270; 08 (2) for three years from the date the judgment is stayed or satisfied 09 if the suspension is the person's second suspension under AS 28.20.270; 10 (3) for 10 years from the date the judgment is stayed or satisfied if 11 the suspension is the person's third suspension under AS 28.20.270; 12 (4) during the period the person has a license or nonresident's 13 operating privilege if the suspension is the person's fourth or subsequent 14 suspension under AS 28.20.270. 01 * Sec. 2. The uncodified law of the State of Alaska is amended by adding a new section to 02 read: 03 APPLICABILITY. AS 28.20.330(b), as amended by sec. 1 of this Act, applies to 04 suspensions occurring before, on, or after the effective date of this Act. 05 * Sec. 3. The uncodified law of the State of Alaska is amended by adding a new section to 06 read: 07 TRANSITION: NOTIFICATION. Within six months after the effective date of this 08 Act, an insurance company doing business in the state shall provide notice of the provisions 09 of this Act to the company's customers in this state who maintain proof of financial 10 responsibility as required under AS 28.20.330.

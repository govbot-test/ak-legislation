and 07 WHEREAS the condition of the disaster emergency was extended in November of 08 2025, December of 2025, and January of 2026, and on February 6, 2026, with the disaster 09 emergency expiring on March 8, 2026; and 10 WHEREAS the effects of the storm remain severe, and infrastructure systems from 11 the original storm, as well as ongoing emergency actions, are necessary to respond to those 12 effects; and 13 WHEREAS, under AS 26.23.020(c), "[a] proclamation of disaster emergency may 14 not remain in effect longer than 30 days unless extended by the legislature by a concurrent 15 resolution"; and 16 WHEREAS an additional extension will allow agencies to continue the agencies' 17 emergency response to meet the disaster; 01 BE IT RESOLVED that, under AS 26.23.020(c), the Alaska State Legislature 02 extends the October 9, 2025, disaster emergency declared by the governor for the 2025 West 03 Coast Storm until April 7, 2026; and be it 04 FURTHER RESOLVED by the Alaska State Legislature that the extension of the 05 October 9, 2025, disaster emergency declared by the governor for the 2025 West Coast Storm 06 be made retroactive to March 8, 2026.

and 06 WHEREAS the condition of the disaster emergency was extended in November of 07 2025, December of 2025, January of 2026, and February 6, 2026, with the disaster emergency 08 expiring on March 8, 2026; and 09 WHEREAS the effects of the storm remain severe, and infrastructure systems from 10 the original storm, as well as ongoing emergency actions, are necessary to respond to those 11 effects; and 12 WHEREAS, under AS 26.23.020(c), "[a] proclamation of disaster emergency may 13 not remain in effect longer than 30 days unless extended by the legislature by a concurrent 14 resolution"; and 15 WHEREAS an additional extension will allow agencies to continue the agencies' 16 emergency response to meet the disaster; 01 BE IT RESOLVED that, under AS 26.23.020(c), the Alaska State Legislature 02 extends the October 9, 2025, disaster emergency declared by the governor for the 2025 West 03 Coast Storm until April 7, 2026; and be it 04 FURTHER RESOLVED by the Alaska State Legislature that the extension of the 05 October 9, 2025, disaster emergency declared by the governor for the 2025 West Coast Storm 06 be made retroactive to March 8, 2026.

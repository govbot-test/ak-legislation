Article IX, sec. 12, Constitution of the State of Alaska, is amended to read: 06 Section 12. Budget. (a) The governor shall submit to the legislature, at a time 07 fixed by law, a budget for the next fiscal year setting forth all proposed expenditures 08 and anticipated income of all departments, offices, and agencies of the State. The 09 governor, at the same time, shall submit a general appropriation bill to authorize the 10 proposed expenditures, and a bill or bills covering recommendations in the budget for 11 new or additional revenues. 12 * Sec. 2. Article IX, sec. 12, Constitution of the State of Alaska, is amended by adding a 13 new subsection to read: 14 (b) The governor may not submit under (a) of this section a budget proposing 15 expenditures or a general appropriation bill authorizing expenditures for the next fiscal 01 year that exceed available fund balances, anticipated revenue of all departments, 02 offices, and agencies of the State for the next fiscal year, and income of the permanent 03 fund available for appropriation as authorized by law. Under this subsection, available 04 fund balances do not include money in the budget reserve fund established under 05 Section 17 of this article or the income of the permanent fund, whether deposited in 06 the general fund or another fund, that exceeds the amount available for appropriation 07 as authorized by law. 08 * Sec. 3. Article XV, Constitution of the State of Alaska, is amended by adding a new 09 section to read: 10 Section 30. Application of Amendment relating to the Governor's Duty to 11 Submit a Balanced Budget. The 2026 amendment relating to the governor's duty to 12 submit a balanced budget (art. IX, sec. 12(b)) applies to budgets submitted by the 13 governor on or after January 1, 2027. 14 * Sec. 4. The amendment proposed by this resolution shall be placed before the voters of the 15 state at the next general election in conformity with art. XIII, sec. 1, Constitution of the State 16 of Alaska, and the election laws of the state.

AS 11.56 is amended by adding a new section to article 6 to read: 05 Sec. 11.56.880. Wearing a mask in public while acting as a peace officer. 06 (a) A peace officer commits the crime of wearing a mask in public while acting as a 07 peace officer if, while interacting with the public in the peace officer's official 08 capacity, the peace officer knowingly conceals the peace officer's face with a mask or 09 disguise. 10 (b) This section does not apply to a peace officer who 11 (1) is on an undercover assignment; 12 (2) is wearing 13 (A) a shield that does not obscure the peace officer's face that is 14 designed to protect the peace officer's face from harm; 01 (B) a medical mask or respirator that is designed to prevent the 02 transmission of disease if the peace officer has obtained a statement from a 03 licensed physician that the mask or respirator is necessary to protect the health 04 of the peace officer; 05 (C) a mask for protection against exposure to smoke. 06 (c) Wearing a mask in public while acting as a peace officer is a class B 07 misdemeanor. 08 * Sec. 2. The uncodified law of the State of Alaska is amended by adding a new section to 09 read: 10 APPLICABILITY. AS 11.56.880, enacted by sec. 1 of this Act, applies to offenses 11 committed on or after the effective date of this Act. 12 * Sec. 3. This Act takes effect immediately under AS 01.10.070(c).

AS 21.03.021 is amended by adding a new subsection to read: 04 (m) This title does not apply to a prepaid legal plan. In this subsection, 05 "prepaid legal plan" means an arrangement between a member and an organization in 06 which 07 (1) the member prepays for specified legal services that are available 08 to the member or the member's dependent; 09 (2) the organization contracts directly with an attorney for the 10 provision of the specified legal services and pays the attorney a fixed, prearranged 11 payment; and 12 (3) an attorney contracted under this subsection does not receive 13 additional payment or reimbursement from the organization for provision of the 14 specified legal services or the specified legal expenses. 15 * Sec. 2. AS 45.50.471(b) is amended by adding a new paragraph to read: 01 (58) violating AS 45.50.476 (prepaid legal plans). 02 * Sec. 3. AS 45.50 is amended by adding a new section to read: 03 Sec. 45.50.476. Prepaid legal plans. (a) A person that offers a prepaid legal 04 plan exempt under AS 21.03.021 may not engage in misleading advertising that 05 implies the prepaid legal plan is insurance or guarantees outcomes beyond the scope of 06 covered services. A person that offers a prepaid legal plan shall 07 (1) before enrollment, provide a written disclosure to members that 08 includes 09 (A) a complete list of covered legal services and any significant 10 exclusions; 11 (B) the cost and payment terms of the plan; 12 (C) information on how to access services and contact 13 information for the plan administrator and participating attorneys; 14 (D) a statement that the plan is not insurance and is not subject 15 to insurance regulation; and 16 (E) the procedures for filing a complaint or resolving a dispute; 17 and 18 (2) maintain records of member complaints for at least three years and 19 make complaints available to the attorney general upon request. 20 (b) In this section, "prepaid legal plan" has the meaning given in 21 AS 21.03.021.

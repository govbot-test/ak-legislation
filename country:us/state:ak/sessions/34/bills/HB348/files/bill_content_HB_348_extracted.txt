AS 03.55 is amended by adding new sections to read: 04 Article 1A. Cats. 05 Sec. 03.55.080. Feral cats. (a) Subject to the approval of the landowner, a 06 person may release a community cat on land from which the cat was originally taken. 07 (b) A person who releases a community cat under this section is exempt from 08 state and municipal requirements applicable to ownership and care of domestic 09 animals and the release of a community cat under (a) of this section does not constitute 10 abandonment. 11 Sec. 03.55.095. Definitions. In AS 03.55.080 - 03.55.095, 12 (1) "community cat" means a feral cat that has been vaccinated for 13 rabies, has had its left ear tip removed, and has been spayed or neutered; 14 (2) "feral cat" means an unowned domestic cat living in a wild state 15 that is unsocialized for domestic human environments; 01 (3) "landowner" includes state, municipal, and private landowners. 02 * Sec. 2. AS 16.05.930 is amended by adding a new subsection to read: 03 (h) This chapter does not prohibit the release of a community cat as allowed 04 under AS 03.55.080.

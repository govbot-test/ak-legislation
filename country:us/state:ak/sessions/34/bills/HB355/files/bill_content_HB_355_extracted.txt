AS 03.10.020(a) is amended to read: 05 (a) The Board of Agriculture and Conservation (AS 03.09.010) may 06 (1) make a loan to 07 (A) an individual resident farmer, homesteader, or a 08 partnership, cooperative, or corporation composed of farmers and 09 homesteaders for 10 (i) clearing land for agricultural purposes; 11 (ii) development of farms; 12 (iii) storage and processing of farm produce; 13 (iv) the purchase of livestock or machinery; 14 (v) in-state manufacturing of food or production of 01 animal feed; or 02 (vi) the cost of shipping to and within the state for the 03 purposes listed in (i) - (v) of this subparagraph; 04 (B) an individual state resident, or a partnership, cooperative, 05 or corporation for 06 (i) storage and processing plants for agricultural 07 products; 08 (ii) the commercial production or processing of 09 horticultural products in the state; 10 (iii) the commercial production or processing of animal 11 feed in the state; 12 (iv) the raising or care of animals in the state for the 13 purpose of marketing their fur; 14 (v) in-state manufacturing of food or production of 15 animal feed; or 16 (vi) the cost of shipping to and within the state for the 17 purposes listed in (i) - (v) of this subparagraph; 18 (2) designate agents and delegate its powers to them as necessary; 19 (3) adopt regulations necessary to carry out its functions, including 20 regulations to establish reasonable fees for services provided and charges for 21 collecting the fees; 22 (4) establish amortization plans for repayment of loans, which may 23 include delayed payments of principal and interest for not to exceed five years; 24 (5) enter into agreements with private lending institutions, other state 25 agencies, or agencies of the federal government to carry out the purposes of this 26 chapter; 27 (6) collect the fees and collection charges established under this 28 subsection; 29 (7) refinance a debt obligation incurred by a borrower under this 30 section or through another lender for a purpose authorized under (1) of this subsection 31 if the borrower otherwise qualifies for a loan under this chapter. 01 * Sec. 2. AS 23.30.230(a) is amended to read: 02 (a) The following persons are not covered by this chapter: 03 (1) a part-time babysitter; 04 (2) a cleaning person; 05 (3) harvest help and similar part-time or transient help; 06 (4) a person employed as a sports official on a contractual basis and 07 who officiates only at sports events in which the players are not compensated; in this 08 paragraph, "sports official" includes an umpire, referee, judge, scorekeeper, 09 timekeeper, organizer, or other person who is a neutral participant in a sports event; 10 (5) a person employed as an entertainer on a contractual basis; 11 (6) a commercial fisherman, as defined in AS 16.05.940; 12 (7) an individual who drives a taxicab and whose compensation and 13 written contractual arrangement is as described in AS 23.10.055(a)(13), unless the 14 hours worked by the individual or the areas in which the individual may work are 15 restricted except to comply with local ordinances; 16 (8) a participant in the Alaska temporary assistance program 17 (AS 47.27) who is engaged in work activities required under AS 47.27.035 other than 18 subsidized or unsubsidized work or on-the-job training; 19 (9) a person employed as a player or coach by a professional hockey 20 team if the person is covered under a health care insurance plan provided by the 21 professional hockey team, the coverage is applicable to both work-related and 22 nonwork-related injuries, and the coverage provides medical and related benefits as 23 required under this chapter, except that coverage may not be limited to two years from 24 the date of injury as described under AS 23.30.095(a); in this paragraph, "health care 25 insurance" has the meaning given in AS 21.12.050; 26 (10) a person working as a qualified real estate licensee who performs 27 services under a written contract that provides that the person will not be treated as an 28 employee for federal income tax or workers' compensation purposes; in this 29 paragraph, "qualified real estate licensee" means a person who is required to be 30 licensed under AS 08.88.161 and whose payment for services is directly related to 31 sales or other output rather than the number of hours worked; 01 (11) a transportation network company driver who provides a 02 prearranged ride or is otherwise logged onto the digital network of a transportation 03 network company as a driver; [AND] 04 (12) a person employed as an independent contractor; a person is an 05 independent contractor for the purposes of this section only if the person 06 (A) has an express contract to perform the services; 07 (B) is free from direction and control over the means and 08 manner of providing services, subject only to the right of the individual for 09 whom, or entity for which, the services are provided to specify the desired 10 results, completion schedule, or range of work hours, or to monitor the work 11 for compliance with contract plans and specifications, or federal, state, or 12 municipal law; 13 (C) incurs most of the expenses for tools, labor, and other 14 operational costs necessary to perform the services, except that materials and 15 equipment may be supplied; 16 (D) has an opportunity for profit and loss as a result of the 17 services performed for the other individual or entity; 18 (E) is free to hire and fire employees to help perform the 19 services for the contracted work; 20 (F) has all business, trade, or professional licenses required by 21 federal, state, or municipal authorities for a business or individual engaging in 22 the same type of services as the person; 23 (G) follows federal Internal Revenue Service requirements by 24 (i) obtaining an employer identification number, if 25 required; 26 (ii) filing business or self-employment tax returns for 27 the previous tax year to report profit or income earned for the same 28 type of services provided under the contract; or 29 (iii) intending to file business or self-employment tax 30 returns for the current tax year to report profit or income earned for the 31 same type of services provided under the contract if the person's 01 business was not operating in the previous tax year; and 02 (H) meets at least two of the following criteria: 03 (i) the person is responsible for the satisfactory 04 completion of services that the person has contracted to perform and is 05 subject to liability for a failure to complete the contracted work, or 06 maintains liability insurance or other insurance policies necessary to 07 protect the employees, financial interests, and customers of the person's 08 business; 09 (ii) the person maintains a business location or a 10 business mailing address separate from the location of the individual 11 for whom, or the entity for which, the services are performed; 12 (iii) the person provides contracted services for two or 13 more different customers within a 12-month period or engages in any 14 kind of business advertising, solicitation, or other marketing efforts 15 reasonably calculated to obtain new contracts to provide similar 16 services; and 17 (13) a person who is a member of an agricultural cooperative; in 18 this paragraph, "agricultural cooperative" means a cooperative that is organized 19 and operated for the purpose of 20 (A) marketing agricultural products of members of the 21 cooperative or of other producers of agricultural products, and providing 22 a member with the proceeds of product sales, less the necessary marketing 23 expenses, based on either the quantity or the value of the products 24 furnished by the member; or 25 (B) purchasing agricultural supplies and equipment for the 26 use of members or other persons, and providing the supplies and 27 equipment to the members at actual cost, plus necessary expenses. 28 * Sec. 3.

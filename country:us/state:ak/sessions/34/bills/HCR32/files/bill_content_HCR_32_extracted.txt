raising the 04 minimum age to purchase, exchange, or possess tobacco, a product containing nicotine, 05 or an electronic smoking product; relating to the tobacco use education and cessation 06 fund; and relating to the taxation of electronic smoking products and vapor products. 07 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF ALASKA: 08 That under Rule 54, Uniform Rules of the Alaska State Legislature, the provisions of 09 Rules 24(c), 35, 41(b), and 42(e), Uniform Rules of the Alaska State Legislature, regarding 10 changes to the title of a bill, are suspended in consideration of Senate Bill No. 24, relating to 11 tobacco, tobacco products, electronic smoking products, nicotine, and products containing 12 nicotine;

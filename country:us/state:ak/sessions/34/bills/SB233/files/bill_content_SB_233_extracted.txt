AS 11.71.100(a) is amended to read: 04 (a) The Controlled Substances Advisory Committee is established in the 05 Department of Commerce, Community, and Economic Development [LAW]. The 06 committee consists of 07 (1) the attorney general or the attorney general's designee; 08 (2) the commissioner of family and community services or the 09 commissioner's designee; 10 (3) the commissioner of public safety or the commissioner's designee; 11 (4) the president of the Board of Pharmacy or the designee of the 12 president who shall also be a member of the Board of Pharmacy; 13 (5) a peace officer appointed by the governor after consultation with 14 the Alaska Association of Chiefs of Police; 15 (6) a physician appointed by the governor; 01 (7) a psychiatrist appointed by the governor; and 02 (8) two individuals appointed by the governor.

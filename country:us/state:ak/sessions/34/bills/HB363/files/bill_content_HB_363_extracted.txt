AS 04.09.220(d) is amended to read: 05 (d) Alcoholic beverages sold under a club license may be purchased only by 06 (1) members of the club and their families; 07 (2) widows or widowers of deceased members of the club who have 08 been accorded club privileges; 09 (3) military personnel on active duty who are extended the privilege by 10 patriotic organizations; and 11 (4) members of a patriotic organization licensed under this section 12 [THAT HAS A RECIPROCITY AGREEMENT WITH ANOTHER PATRIOTIC 13 ORGANIZATION LICENSED UNDER THIS SECTION]. 14 * Sec. 2. AS 04.09.660(a) is amended to read: 01 (a) A nonprofit organization event permit authorizes the holder to sell or 02 dispense brewed beverages or wine for consumption at an event organized by the 03 nonprofit organization, except that a holder that is a patriotic organization may 04 also sell or dispense other alcoholic beverages for consumption at an event 05 organized by the patriotic organization. 06 * Sec. 3. AS 04.09.660(e) is amended to read: 07 (e) The nonprofit organization shall ensure that all profits derived from the 08 sale of brewed beverages, [OR] wine, or other alcoholic beverages, as applicable, 09 under a nonprofit organization event permit are paid to the organization that holds the 10 permit and not to an individual. 11 * Sec. 4. AS 04.21.080(b) is amended by adding a new paragraph to read: 12 (31) "patriotic organization" includes the American Legion, Veterans 13 of Foreign Wars, and American Veterans organizations. 14 * Sec. 5. AS 04.09.220(j) is repealed. 15 * Sec. 6. This Act takes effect immediately under AS 01.10.070(c).

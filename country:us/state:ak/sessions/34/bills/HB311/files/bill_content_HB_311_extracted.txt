AS 28.10.181 is amended by adding a new subsection to read: 05 (pp) Special request plates commemorating the 250th anniversary of the 06 United States of America. Upon application by the owner of a motor vehicle, the 07 department may issue registration plates commemorating the 250th anniversary of the 08 United States of America. The commissioner shall determine the design and color of 09 the plates. The department may disapprove the issuance of registration plates under 10 this subsection when the requested plates are a duplication of an existing registration. 11 * Sec. 2. This Act takes effect immediately under AS 01.10.070(c).

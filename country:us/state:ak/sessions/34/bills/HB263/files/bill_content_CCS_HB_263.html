21 number of qualified entities with current capacity and willingness to accept Alaska inmates,                            
22 including the number of beds available at each facility, the timeframe in which such capacity                           
23 could be made available, and the inmate classification levels and offense types each entity is                          
24 willing and authorized to accept, including any exclusions based on custody level, violent                              
25 offense history, medical or mental health needs, or other eligibility restrictions.

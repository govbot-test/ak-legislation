AS 37.14 is amended by adding a new section to read: 04 Article 12. Opioid Litigation Settlement Fund. 05 Sec. 37.14.900. Opioid litigation settlement fund established. (a) The opioid 06 litigation settlement fund is established as a separate fund in the state treasury. The 07 legislature may appropriate to the fund settlement proceeds received by the state from 08 manufacturers or distributors of opioids and income from the fund. 09 (b) The Department of Revenue may spend money in the fund for opioid 10 remediation in compliance with settlement agreements between the state and 11 manufacturers or distributors of opioids and to pay the costs to manage the fund. 12 (c) Money in the fund does not lapse. 13 (d) Nothing in this section creates a dedicated fund.

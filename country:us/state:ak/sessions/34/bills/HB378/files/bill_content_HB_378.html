HB 378: "An Act relating to the Public Employees' Retirement System of Alaska and the teachers' retirement system; and providing for an effective date." 

00                            HOUSE BILL NO. 378                                                                          
01 "An Act relating to the Public Employees' Retirement System of Alaska and the                                           
02 teachers' retirement system; and providing for an effective date."                                                      
03 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF ALASKA:                                                                
04    * Section 1. AS 14.25.009 is amended to read:                                                                      
05 Sec. 14.25.009. Applicability of AS 14.25.009 - 14.25.220. The provisions of                                          
06 AS 14.25.009 - 14.25.220 apply only to members first hired before July 1, 2006, and                                 
07 teachers who are also members of the public employees' defined benefit                                              
08 retirement plan under AS 39.35.095 - 39.35.680 who do not elect under                                               
09 AS 14.25.540 to participate in the defined contribution retirement plan under                                       
10       AS 14.25.310 - 14.25.590.                                                                                     
11    * Sec. 2. AS 14.25.065(b) is amended to read:                                                                    
12 (b)  The contributions of employers under AS 14.25.070 must be transmitted to                                           
13 the plan for deposit in the retirement fund and the Alaska retiree health care trust at the                             
14 close of each pay period. If the contributions are not submitted within the prescribed                                  



01 time limit, interest must be assessed on the outstanding contributions for a period not                             
02 to exceed six months at a rate equal to [ONE AND ONE-HALF TIMES] the most                                       
03 recent actuarially determined rate of earnings for the plan from the date that                                          
04 contributions were originally due. Amounts due from an employer and interest as                                         
05 prescribed in this section may be claimed by the administrator from any agency of the                                   
06 state or political subdivision that has in its possession funds of the employer or that is                              
07 authorized to disburse funds to the employer that are not restricted by statute or                                      
08 appropriation to a specific purpose. The amount claimed shall be certified by the                                       
09 administrator as sufficient to pay the contributions and interest due from the employer.                                
10 The amount claimed shall be submitted to the administrator for deposit in the                                           
11       retirement fund and the Alaska retiree health care trust.                                                         
12    * Sec. 3. AS 14.25.070(a) is amended to read:                                                                      
13 (a)  Each employer shall contribute to the system every payroll period the                                          
14       lesser of                                                                                                     
15 (1)  an amount calculated by applying a rate of 12.56 percent to the                                                
16 total of all base salaries paid by the employer to active members of the system and to                                  
17 members who are retired from the plan and reemployed under AS 14.20.136,                                                
18       including any adjustments to contributions required by AS 14.25.173(a); or                                    
19                 (2)  an amount adopted by the board under AS 37.10.220.                                             
20    * Sec. 4. AS 14.25.310 is amended to read:                                                                         
21 Sec. 14.25.310. Applicability of AS 14.25.310 - 14.25.590. The provisions of                                          
22 AS 14.25.310 - 14.25.590 apply only to teachers who first become members on or                                          
23 after July 1, 2006, to members who are employed by employers that do not participate                                    
24 in the defined benefit retirement plan established under AS 14.25.009 - 14.25.220, to                               
25 teachers who are also members of the defined benefit retirement plan under                                          
26 AS 39.35.095 - 39.35.680 and elect to participate in this plan [TO FORMER                                           
27 MEMBERS UNDER AS 14.25.220], or to members who transfer into the defined                                                
28       contribution retirement plan under AS 14.25.540.                                                                
29    * Sec. 5. AS 14.25.330(a) is amended to read:                                                                      
30 (a)  A teacher who first becomes a member on or after July 1, 2006, and who                                         
31 is not also a member of the defined benefit retirement plan under AS 39.35.095 -                                    



01       39.35.680 shall participate in the system [PLAN] as a member of the defined                               
02       contribution retirement plan.                                                                                     
03    * Sec. 6. AS 14.25.470 is amended by adding a new subsection to read:                                              
04            (h)  Notwithstanding (a) and (g) of this section, a disabled employee who is                                 
05       eligible to obtain a disability benefit under AS 14.25.483 or 14.25.485 is eligible to                            
06       obtain retiree major medical insurance benefits under AS 14.25.480.                                               
07    * Sec. 7. AS 14.25.480(g) is amended to read:                                                                      
08            (g)  Except as provided under (l) of this section, the [THE] cost of                                   
09       premiums for retiree major medical insurance coverage for an eligible member or                                   
10       surviving spouse who is                                                                                           
11 (1)  not eligible for Medicare is an amount equal to the full monthly                                                   
12       group premiums for retiree major medical insurance coverage;                                                      
13 (2)  eligible for Medicare is the following percentage of the premium                                                   
14       amounts established for retirees who are eligible for Medicare:                                                   
15 (A)  30 percent if the member had 10 or more, but less than 15,                                                         
16            years of service;                                                                                            
17 (B)  25 percent if the member had 15 or more, but less than 20,                                                         
18            years of service;                                                                                            
19 (C)  20 percent if the member had 20 or more, but less than 25,                                                         
20            years of service;                                                                                            
21 (D)  15 percent if the member had 25 or more, but less than 30,                                                         
22            years of service;                                                                                            
23                      (E)  10 percent if the member had 30 or more years of service.                                     
24    * Sec. 8. AS 14.25.480 is amended by adding a new subsection to read:                                              
25 (l)  An eligible member or surviving spouse who receives a disability benefit as                                        
26 provided under AS 14.25.483 or 14.25.485 and is not eligible for Medicare is not                                        
27       required to pay a premium for retiree major medical insurance coverage.                                           
28    * Sec. 9. AS 14.25 is amended by adding a new section to read:                                                     
29 Sec. 14.25.483. Nonoccupational disability benefits. (a) A member who has                                             
30 10 or more years of credited service is eligible for a nonoccupational disability benefit                               
31 if the member's employment is terminated because of a total and apparently permanent                                    



01       disability caused by a nonoccupational injury or illness, and the disability occurs                               
02       before the member's normal retirement age.                                                                        
03            (b)  The monthly amount of a nonoccupational disability benefit is 40 percent                                
04       of the disabled member's gross monthly compensation for the last full month the                                   
05       member was employed before termination because of disability.                                                     
06            (c)  While a member is receiving a nonoccupational disability benefit, the                                   
07       employer shall contribute monthly                                                                                 
08                 (1)  to the member's individual account under AS 14.25.340; and                                         
09                 (2)  to the trust account as provided under AS 14.25.350(e) in an                                       
10 amount calculated by using the member's gross compensation as determined under (b)                                      
11       of this section, without a deduction for a disability benefit.                                                    
12 (d)  A member receiving a nonoccupational disability benefit may not elect                                              
13 distribution under AS 14.25.410 of all or a portion of the member's individual account.                                 
14 (e)  A nonoccupational disability benefit terminates the last day of the month in                                       
15       which the member                                                                                                  
16                 (1)  dies;                                                                                              
17                 (2)  reaches normal retirement age; or                                                                  
18                 (3)  is no longer totally and permanently disabled.                                                     
19 (f)  When a member reaches normal retirement age, the member's retirement                                               
20 eligibility and benefits shall be determined as if the member was an active member of                                   
21 the plan while receiving the nonoccupational disability benefit. In this subsection,                                    
22       "active member" has the meaning given in AS 14.25.220.                                                            
23    * Sec. 10. AS 39.35.120(b) is amended to read:                                                                     
24 (b)  Inclusion in the plan is a condition of employment for an employee except                                          
25       as otherwise provided for                                                                                         
26                 (1)  an elected official;                                                                               
27                 (2)  [REPEALED                                                                                          
28 (3)]  an employee of the university who participates in a university                                                    
29       retirement program under AS 14.40.661 - 14.40.799;                                                            
30 (3)  an employee who is a member of a defined contribution                                                          
31       retirement plan under AS 14.25.310 - 14.25.590 or AS 39.35.700 - 39.35.990.                                   



01    * Sec. 11. AS 39.35.255(a) is amended to read:                                                                     
02            (a)  Each employer, except as provided in (h) of this section, shall contribute to                           
03       the system every payroll period the lesser of                                                                 
04                 (1)  an amount calculated by applying a rate of 22 percent of the greater                           
05       of                                                                                                                
06                      (A)  the total of all base salaries [(1)] paid by the employer to                              
07            employees who are active members of the system, including any adjustments                                    
08            to contributions required by AS 39.35.520; or                                                                
09                      (B)  an amount calculated by applying a rate of 75 percent                                     
10 of the annual average total of all base salaries [(2)] paid by the employer                                         
11 over the immediately preceding five fiscal years to employees who were                                              
12 active members of the system during that [THE CORRESPONDING                                                         
13            PAYROLL] period, as determined by the board; or                                                          
14 (2)  an amount adopted by the board under AS 37.10.220 [FOR                                                         
15       THE FISCAL YEAR ENDING                                                                                            
16                      (A)  JUNE 30, 2008; OR                                                                             
17 (B)  JUNE 30, 2012, IF THAT TOTAL IS LESS THAN THE                                                                      
18 TOTAL UNDER (A) OF THIS PARAGRAPH, AND THE EMPLOYER IS A                                                                
19 MUNICIPALITY IN WHICH THE POPULATION DECREASED BY                                                                       
20 MORE THAN 25 PERCENT BETWEEN 2000 AND 2010, ACCORDING                                                                   
21 TO THE DECENNIAL CENSUS CONDUCTED BY THE UNITED STATES                                                                  
22            BUREAU OF THE CENSUS].                                                                                       
23    * Sec. 12. AS 39.35.300 is amended by adding a new subsection to read:                                             
24 (f)  Notwithstanding AS 39.35.330 and (b) of this section, a seasonal employee                                          
25 shall receive one year of credited service for each period of 12 consecutive months in                                  
26 which the employee works not less than 1,560 hours. In this subsection, "seasonal                                       
27       employee" does not include a temporary employee of the legislature.                                               
28    * Sec. 13. AS 39.35.610(a) is amended to read:                                                                     
29 (a)  The contributions of an employer and the contributions of its employees                                            
30 shall be transmitted to the administrator as soon as practicable after the close of the                                 
31 payroll period for which the contributions are made. Subject to (c) of this section, if an                              



01 employer is delinquent in transferring the contributions for more than 15 days, interest                                
02 shall be assessed on the outstanding contributions for a period not to exceed six                                   
03 months at a rate equal to [ONE AND ONE-HALF TIMES] the most recent                                              
04 actuarially determined rate of earnings for the retirement plan from the date that the                                  
05       contributions were originally due.                                                                                
06    * Sec. 14. AS 39.35.680(22) is amended to read:                                                                    
07                 (22)  "member" or "employee"                                                                            
08                      (A)  means a person eligible to participate in the plan and who                                    
09            is covered by the plan;                                                                                      
10                      (B)  includes                                                                                      
11                           (i)  an active member;                                                                        
12                           (ii)  an inactive member;                                                                     
13                           (iii)  a vested member;                                                                       
14                           (iv)  a deferred vested member;                                                               
15                           (v)  a nonvested member;                                                                      
16                           (vi)  a disabled member;                                                                      
17                           (vii)  a retired member;                                                                      
18                           (viii)  an elected public officer under AS 39.35.381;                                         
19                      (C)  does not include                                                                              
20                           (i)  former members;                                                                          
21                           (ii)  persons compensated on a contractual or fee basis;                                      
22 (iii)  casual or emergency workers or nonpermanent                                                                      
23                 employees as defined in AS 39.25.200;                                                                   
24 (iv)  persons covered by the Alaska Teachers'                                                                           
25 Retirement System except as provided under AS 14.25.009,                                                            
26 AS 39.35.131 and 39.35.381, or persons covered by a university                                                          
27                 retirement program;                                                                                     
28 (v)  employees of the division of marine transportation                                                                 
29 engaged in operating the state ferry system who are covered by a union                                                  
30                 or group retirement system to which the state makes contributions;                                      
31 (vi)  justices of the supreme court or judges of the court                                                              



01                 of appeals or of the superior or district courts of Alaska;                                             
02                           (vii)  the administrative director of the Alaska Court                                        
03                 System appointed under art. IV, sec. 16 of the state constitution unless                                
04                 the director becomes a member under AS 39.35.158;                                                       
05                           (viii)  members of the elected public officers' retirement                                    
06                 system (former AS 39.37); and                                                                           
07                           (ix)  contractual employees of the legislative branch of                                      
08                 state government under AS 24.10.060(f);                                                                 
09                      (D)  may include employees of the division of marine                                               
10 transportation excluded under (C)(v) of this paragraph if [PROVIDED THAT]                                           
11 (i)  the State of Alaska formally agrees to their inclusion                                                             
12                 through the process of collective bargaining; and                                                       
13 (ii)  no collective bargaining agreement has the effect of                                                              
14 obligating contributions made by the state under AS 39.30.150 in the                                                    
15 event the state resumes participation in the federal social security                                                    
16                 system;                                                                                                 
17    * Sec. 15. AS 39.35.870 is amended by adding a new subsection to read:                                             
18 (h)  Notwithstanding (a) and (g) of this section, a disabled employee who is                                            
19 eligible to obtain a disability benefit under AS 39.35.888 or 39.35.890 is eligible to                                  
20       obtain retiree major medical insurance benefits under AS 39.35.880.                                               
21    * Sec. 16. AS 39.35.880(g) is amended to read:                                                                     
22 (g)  Except as provided under (l) of this section, the [THE] cost of                                              
23 premiums for retiree major medical insurance coverage for an eligible member or                                         
24       surviving spouse who is                                                                                           
25 (1)  not eligible for Medicare is an amount equal to the full monthly                                                   
26       group premiums for retiree major medical insurance coverage;                                                      
27 (2)  eligible for Medicare is the following percentage of the premium

08            (l)  An eligible member or surviving spouse who receives a disability benefit as                             
09       provided under AS 39.35.888, 39.35.890, or 39.35.891 and is not eligible for Medicare                             
10       is not required to pay a premium for retiree major medical insurance coverage.                                    
11    * Sec. 18. AS 39.35 is amended by adding a new section to read:                                                    
12 Sec. 39.35.888. Nonoccupational disability benefits. (a) A member who has                                             
13 10 or more years of credited service is eligible for a nonoccupational disability benefit                               
14 if the member's employment is terminated because of a total and apparently permanent                                    
15 disability caused by a nonoccupational injury or illness, and the disability occurs                                     
16       before the member's normal retirement age.                                                                        
17 (b)  The monthly amount of a nonoccupational disability benefit is 40 percent                                           
18 of the disabled member's gross compensation for the last full month the member was                                      
19       employed.                                                                                                         
20 (c)  While a member is receiving a nonoccupational disability benefit, the                                              
21       employer shall contribute monthly                                                                                 
22                 (1)  to the member's individual account under AS 39.35.730; and                                         
23 (2)  to the trust account as provided under AS 39.35.750(e) in an                                                       
24 amount calculated by using the member's monthly compensation for the last full                                          
25       month the member was employed before termination because of disability.                                           
26 (d)  A member receiving a nonoccupational disability benefit may not elect                                              
27       distribution under AS 39.35.810 of all or a portion of the member's individual account.

29       which the employee                                                                                                
30                 (1)  dies;

07 read:                                                                                                                   
08       TRANSITION: RETIREMENT PLAN ELECTION. A teacher who became a member                                               
09 of the defined contribution retirement plan under AS 14.25.310 - 14.25.590 after June 30,                               
10 2006, and before the effective date of this Act, and who, on the effective date of this Act, is a                       
11 member employed by an employer in that defined contribution retirement plan and is also a                               
12 member of the defined benefit retirement plan under AS 39.35.095 - 39.35.680 may, within                                
13 180 days after the effective date of this Act, make a one-time election to participate in the                           
14 defined benefit retirement plan under AS 14.25.009 - 14.25.220 and to transfer all                                      
15 contributions that have been made or should be made to the defined contribution retirement                              
16 plan for service the member completes before the effective date of the member's participation                           
17 in the defined benefit retirement plan. The transferred contributions shall be used to purchase                         
18 credited service in the defined benefit retirement plan under AS 14.25.009 - 14.25.220 on an                            
19 actuarial equivalent basis determined by the Alaska Retirement Management Board                                         
20 established under AS 37.10.210. The provisions of AS 14.25.044 apply to an election made                                
21 under this subsection.                                                                                                  
22    * Sec. 20. This Act takes effect July 1, 2026.

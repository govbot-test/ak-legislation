AS 17.20.332(b) is amended to read: 04 (b) The sale of a homemade food under this section 05 (1) is only for personal consumption; 06 (2) must occur in the state at a farmers' market, an agricultural fair, a 07 farm, a ranch, the producer's home or office, the retail location of a third-party seller, 08 or a location agreed on between the producer and the buyer; and 09 (3) may not involve 10 (A) interstate commerce; or 11 (B) the purchase or sale of 12 (i) meat or meat products, except as provided in (h) of 13 this section; 14 (ii) seafood; 15 (iii) a controlled substance; 01 (iv) oil rendered from animal fat; [OR] 02 (v) game meat; or 03 (vi) potentially hazardous homemade food in 04 reduced oxygen packaging. 05 * Sec. 2. AS 17.20.338 is amended by adding a new paragraph to read: 06 (13) "reduced oxygen packaging" means packaging for a homemade 07 food product from which oxygen has been removed to maintain product quality or to 08 promote a vacuum seal.

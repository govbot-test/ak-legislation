The uncodified law of the State of Alaska is amended by adding a new section 02 to read: 03 LEGISLATIVE FINDINGS AND INTENT. (a) The legislature finds that the tax 04 treatment in sec. 30 of this Act is necessary to advance a major natural gas project and to 05 ensure that 06 (1) the project maximizes the benefit to the state by ensuring direct and 07 affordable access to natural gas to the residents of the state; and 08 (2) communities affected by the natural gas project are protected from the 09 negative effects of the project. 10 (b) The legislature finds that, given that the state is part owner of the natural gas 11 project, the project labor agreement, prevailing wage, and apprenticeship utilization 12 requirements in this Act are necessary to advance the project, avoid unnecessary interruptions, 13 and ensure efficient completion, predictable labor costs, a steady supply of skilled labor, high 14 quality training, coordination among multiple employers, and certainty about terms and 15 conditions of employment. 16 (c) Nothing in this Act is intended to establish, modify, impair, waive, or otherwise 17 affect the tax treatment, assessment methodology, valuation, taxing authority, or applicability 18 of taxes imposed under AS 29.45 or AS 43.56 with respect to any other property, project, 19 facility, infrastructure, or taxpayer. The uncodified law of the State of Alaska is amended by adding a new section 02 to read: 03 LEGISLATIVE FINDINGS AND INTENT. (a) The legislature finds that the tax 04 treatment in sec. 30 of this Act is necessary to advance a major natural gas project and to 05 ensure that 06 (1) the project maximizes the benefit to the state by ensuring direct and 07 affordable access to natural gas to the residents of the state; and 08 (2) communities affected by the natural gas project are protected from the 09 negative effects of the project. 10 (b) The legislature finds that, given that the state is part owner of the natural gas 11 project, the project labor agreement, prevailing wage, and apprenticeship utilization 12 requirements in this Act are necessary to advance the project, avoid unnecessary interruptions, 13 and ensure efficient completion, predictable labor costs, a steady supply of skilled labor, high 14 quality training, coordination among multiple employers, and certainty about terms and 15 conditions of employment. 16 (c) Nothing in this Act is intended to establish, modify, impair, waive, or otherwise 17 affect the tax treatment, assessment methodology, valuation, taxing authority, or applicability 18 of taxes imposed under AS 29.45 or AS 43.56 with respect to any other property, project, 19 facility, infrastructure, or taxpayer.

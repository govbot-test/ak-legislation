The uncodified law of the State of Alaska is amended by adding a new section 05 to read: 06 FINDINGS. The legislature finds that 07 (1) on October 9, 2025, the governor declared a disaster emergency, following 08 the 2025 West Coast Storm; 09 (2) the condition of the disaster emergency was extended in November of 10 2025, December of 2025, January of 2026, February of 2026, March of 2026, and April of 11 2026, with the disaster emergency expiring on May 7, 2026; 12 (3) the effects of the storm remains severe, and infrastructure systems from the 13 original storm, as well as ongoing emergency actions, are necessary to respond to those 14 effects; 01 (4) under AS 26.23.020(c), "[a] proclamation of disaster emergency may not 02 remain in effect longer than 30 days unless extended by the legislature by a concurrent 03 resolution"; and 04 (5) an additional extension will allow agencies to continue the agencies' 05 emergency response to meet the disaster. 06 * Sec. 2. The uncodified law of the State of Alaska is amended by adding a new section to 07 read: 08 WEST COAST STORM DISASTER EMERGENCY: APPROVAL, 09 RATIFICATION, AND EXTENSION OF DISASTER EMERGENCY. (a) The declarations 10 of a disaster emergency and financing plans for the 2025 West Coast Storm issued by the 11 governor in November of 2025, December of 2025, January of 2026, February of 2026, 12 March of 2026, and April of 2026, are approved and ratified. 13 (b) Under AS 26.23.020(c), the Alaska State Legislature extends the October 9, 2025, 14 disaster emergency declared by the governor for the 2025 West Coast Storm until June 30, 15 2026. 16 * Sec. 3. The uncodified law of the State of Alaska is amended by adding a new section to 17 read: 18 RETROACTIVITY. Section 2(b) of this Act is retroactive to May 7, 2026. 19 * Sec. 4. This Act takes effect immediately under AS 01.10.070(c).

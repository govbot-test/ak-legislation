AS 28.35.060(b) is amended to read: 04 (b) Except as provided in (c) or (d) of this section, a person who fails to 05 comply with any of the requirements of this section is, upon conviction, punishable by 06 imprisonment for not more than one year, or by a fine of not more than $500, or by 07 both. This provision does not apply to a person incapacitated by the accident to the 08 extent that the person is physically incapable of complying with the requirement. 09 * Sec. 2. AS 28.35.060(c) is amended to read: 10 (c) A person who fails to comply with a requirement of this section regarding 11 assisting an injured person is, upon conviction, guilty of a class B felony, punishable 12 as provided in AS 12.55 [BY IMPRISONMENT FOR NOT MORE THAN 10 13 YEARS, OR BY A FINE OF NOT MORE THAN $10,000, OR BY BOTH]. This 14 provision does not apply to a person incapacitated by the accident to the extent that the 15 person is physically incapable of complying with the requirement. 01 * Sec. 3. AS 28.35.060 is amended by adding a new subsection to read: 02 (d) A person who fails to comply with a requirement of this section regarding 03 failure to render reasonable assistance is, upon conviction, guilty of a class A felony 04 punishable as provided in AS 12.55 and for a first felony conviction shall be sentenced 05 to a presumptive term of imprisonment of seven to 11 years, if the person caused the 06 death of another person while operating a motor vehicle and is convicted of criminally 07 negligent homicide under AS 11.41.130 or of criminally negligent homicide of an 08 unborn child under AS 11.41.170. 09 * Sec. 4. The uncodified law of the State of Alaska is amended by adding a new section to 10 read: 11 APPLICABILITY. AS 28.35.060(b), as amended by sec. 1 of this Act, 12 AS 28.35.060(c), as amended by sec. 2 of this Act, and AS 28.35.060(d), enacted by sec. 3 of 13 this Act, apply to offenses committed on or after the effective date of this Act.

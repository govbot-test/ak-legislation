CONSTITUTIONAL BUDGET RESERVE FUND. (a) If the unrestricted state 02 revenue available for appropriation in fiscal year 2026 is insufficient to cover the general fund 03 appropriations that take effect in fiscal year 2026, the amount necessary to balance revenue 04 and general fund appropriations that take effect in fiscal year 2026 or to prevent a cash 05 deficiency in the general fund in fiscal year 2026, not to exceed $382,300,000, is appropriated 06 to the general fund from the budget reserve fund (art. IX, sec. 17, Constitution of the State of 07 Alaska) as follows: 08 (1) the amount necessary to balance revenue and general fund appropriations 09 that take effect in fiscal year 2026 that were passed by the Thirty-Fourth Alaska State 10 Legislature in the First Regular Session and enacted into law; 11 (2) the amount necessary, after the appropriation made in (1) of this 12 subsection, to balance revenue and general fund appropriations that take effect in fiscal year 13 2026 that are made in this Act, as passed by the Thirty-Fourth Alaska State Legislature in the 14 Second Regular Session and enacted into law; 15 (3) the amount necessary, after the appropriations made in (1) and (2) of this 16 subsection, to balance revenue and all other general fund appropriations that take effect in 17 fiscal year 2026 and to prevent a cash deficiency in the general fund in fiscal year 2026. 18 (b) The appropriations made from the budget reserve fund (art. IX, sec. 17, 19 Constitution of the State of Alaska) in (a) of this section are made under art. IX, sec. 17(c), 20 Constitution of the State of Alaska. 21 * Sec. 11. LAPSE. (a) The appropriations made in secs. 4 - 7 of this Act are for capital 22 projects and lapse under AS 37.25.020. 23 (b) The appropriations made in secs. 8 and 9 of this Act are for the capitalization of 24 funds and do not lapse. 25 * Sec. 12. Sections 7 and 9(a) and (b) of this Act take effect June 30, 2026. 26 * Sec. 13. Except as provided in sec. 12 of this Act, this Act takes effect immediately under 27 AS 01.10.070(c).

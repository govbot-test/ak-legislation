AS 39.35.255(d) is amended to read: 05 (d) Notwithstanding (a) of this section, the annual employer contribution rate 06 may not be less than the rate sufficient to allow payment of 07 (1) the employer normal cost; 08 (2) an amount calculated at the rate certified under 09 AS 37.10.220(a)(8) for liquidating past service liability of the plan; and 10 (3) the employer contributions required under AS 39.30.370 and 11 AS 39.35.750. 12 * Sec. 2. This Act takes effect immediately under AS 01.10.070(c).

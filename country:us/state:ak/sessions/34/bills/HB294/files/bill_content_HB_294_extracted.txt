AS 21.36 is amended by adding a new section to article 5 to read: 04 Sec. 21.36.530. Health care insurance deductibles. An insurer that offers, 05 issues for delivery, delivers, or renews a health care insurance policy may not require 06 an annual deductible that exceeds 07 (1) $1,700 for an individual plan; or 08 (2) $3,400 for a family plan.

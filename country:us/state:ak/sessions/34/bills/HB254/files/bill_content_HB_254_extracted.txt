AS 29.45.110 is amended by adding a new subsection to read: 04 (f) The assessor may not increase the assessed value of real property by more 05 than five percent over the previous year's assessed value unless the increase is based 06 on an improvement to the real property or information about the real property that the 07 assessor did not have at the time of the previous assessment.

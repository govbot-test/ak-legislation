and approving an amended financing plan. 00Enrolled SCR 12 01 Extending the governor's October 9, 2025, declaration of a disaster emergency in response to 02 the 2025 West Coast Storm; and approving an amended financing plan. 03 _______________ 04 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF ALASKA: 05 WHEREAS, on October 9, 2025, the governor declared a disaster emergency, 06 following the 2025 West Coast Storm; and 07 WHEREAS the condition of the disaster emergency was extended in November of 08 2025, December of 2025, and January of 2026, with the disaster emergency expiring on 09 February 6, 2026; and 10 WHEREAS the effects of the storm remain severe, and infrastructure systems from 11 the original storm, as well as ongoing emergency actions, are necessary to respond to those 12 effects; and 13 WHEREAS, under AS 26.23.020(c), "[a] proclamation of disaster emergency may 14 not remain in effect longer than 30 days unless extended by the legislature by a concurrent 15 resolution"; and 16 WHEREAS an additional extension will allow agencies to continue the agencies' 17 emergency response to meet the disaster; and 01 WHEREAS, under AS 26.23.020(k)(1), the governor may expend more than 02 $1,000,000 of state funds to cope with a disaster if, when the legislature is in session, the 03 legislature approves a financing plan to cope with the incident or disaster; and 04 WHEREAS, on January 28, 2026, the governor submitted a revised financing plan to 05 the legislature, totaling $39,250,000 and detailing the estimated shared costs for the 2025 06 West Coast Storm Disaster as $18,750,000 in federal authority and $20,500,000 in state 07 general funds; and 08 WHEREAS the revised financing plan reflects an increase of $5,500,000 in disaster 09 relief funds from the financing plan previously authorized for the 2025 West Coast Storm 10 Disaster; and 11 WHEREAS the current balance of the disaster relief fund established in AS 26.23.300 12 is sufficient to meet the increase in the governor's revised financing plan; 13 BE IT RESOLVED that, under AS 26.23.020(c), the Alaska State Legislature 14 extends the October 9, 2025, disaster emergency declared by the governor for the 2025 West 15 Coast Storm until March 8, 2026; and be it 16 FURTHER RESOLVED by the Alaska State Legislature that the extension of the 17 October 9, 2025, disaster emergency declared by the governor for the 2025 West Coast Storm 18 be made retroactive to February 6, 2026; and be it 19 FURTHER RESOLVED that the Alaska State Legislature approves the increase of 20 $5,500,000 in disaster relief funds in the amended financing plan submitted by the governor 21 for the 2025 West Coast Storm.

The uncodified law of the State of Alaska is amended by adding a new section 06 to read: 07 SHORT TITLE. This Act may be known as the Alaska Heartbeat Act. 08 * Sec. 2. The uncodified law of the State of Alaska is amended by adding a new section to 09 read: 10 LEGISLATIVE FINDINGS. The legislature finds that, according to contemporary 11 medical research, 12 (1) fetal heartbeat has become a key medical predictor that an unborn child 13 will reach live birth; 14 (2) cardiac activity begins at a biologically identifiable moment in time, 01 normally when the fetal heart is formed in the gestational sac; 02 (3) the state has compelling interests from the outset of a woman's pregnancy 03 in protecting the health of the woman and the life of the unborn child; and 04 (4) to make an informed choice about whether to continue her pregnancy, a 05 woman has a compelling interest in knowing the likelihood of her unborn child surviving to 06 live birth based on the presence of cardiac activity. 07 * Sec. 3.

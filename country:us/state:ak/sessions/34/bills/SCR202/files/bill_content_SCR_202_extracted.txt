SCR 202: Suspending Rules 47 and 55, Uniform Rules of the Alaska State Legislature, relating to the carry-over of bills to a special session. 00 SENATE CONCURRENT RESOLUTION NO. 202 01 Suspending Rules 47 and 55, Uniform Rules of the Alaska State Legislature, relating to 02 the carry-over of bills to a special session. 03 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF ALASKA: 04 That under Rule 54, Uniform Rules of the Alaska State Legislature, the provisions of 05 Rule 47 of the Uniform Rules regarding the carry-over of bills and the provisions of Rule 55 06 of the Uniform Rules regarding the use of Mason's Manual of Legislative Procedure, 2020 07 edition, to cover situations not directly addressed by the Uniform Rules, are suspended to the 08 extent they prohibit the carry-over of bills and resolutions from the Second Regular Session to 09 the Second Special Session of the Thirty-Fourth Alaska State Legislature; and be it 10 FURTHER RESOLVED that this resolution applies only to the following bills 11 previously under consideration during the Second Regular Session of the Thirty-Fourth 12 Alaska State Legislature that relate to the subjects set out in the governor's proclamation for 13 the special session: 14 House Bill 381: An Act relating to the taxation of certain natural gas pipeline 15 property; relating to municipal taxation limitations; establishing an alternative 16 volumetric tax on natural gas throughput; relating to the allocation of revenue from the 01 alternative volumetric tax; and providing for an effective date; 02 Senate Bill 280: An Act relating to the taxation of certain natural gas pipeline 03 property; relating to municipal taxation limitations; establishing an alternative 04 volumetric tax on natural gas throughput; relating to the allocation of revenue from the 05 alternative volumetric tax; and providing for an effective date; and be it 06 FURTHER RESOLVED that this resolution is retroactive to May 21, 2026, at 10:00 07 a.m., the beginning of the Second Special Session of the Thirty-Fourth Alaska State 08 Legislature.

AS 39.35.410(d) is amended to read: 05 (d) The monthly amount of an occupational disability benefit is 75 [40] 06 percent of the disabled employee's gross monthly compensation at the time of 07 termination due to disability. 08 * Sec. 2. AS 39.35.870 is amended by adding a new subsection to read: 09 (h) Notwithstanding (a) and (g) of this section, a disabled employee who is 10 eligible to obtain a disability benefit under AS 39.35.890 is eligible to obtain retiree 11 major medical insurance benefits under AS 39.35.880. 12 * Sec. 3. AS 39.35.880(g) is amended to read: 13 (g) Except as provided under (l) of this section, the [THE] cost of 14 premiums for retiree major medical insurance coverage for an eligible member or 01 surviving spouse who is 02 (1) not eligible for Medicare is an amount equal to the full monthly 03 group premiums for retiree major medical insurance coverage; 04 (2) eligible for Medicare is the following percentage of the premium 05 amounts established for retirees who are eligible for Medicare: 06 (A) 30 percent if the member had 10 or more, but less than 15, 07 years of service; 08 (B) 25 percent if the member had 15 or more, but less than 20, 09 years of service; 10 (C) 20 percent if the member had 20 or more, but less than 25, 11 years of service; 12 (D) 15 percent if the member had 25 or more, but less than 30, 13 years of service; 14 (E) 10 percent if the member had 30 or more years of service. 15 * Sec. 4. AS 39.35.880 is amended by adding a new subsection to read: 16 (l) An eligible member or surviving spouse who receives a disability benefit as 17 provided under AS 39.35.890 or 39.35.891 and is not eligible for Medicare is not 18 required to pay a premium for retiree major medical insurance coverage. 19 * Sec. 5. AS 39.35.890(d) is amended to read: 20 (d) The monthly amount of an occupational disability benefit is 75 [40] 21 percent of the disabled employee's gross monthly compensation at the time of 22 termination due to disability. Notwithstanding AS 39.35.790(b), at the time a member 23 is appointed to disability, the member becomes fully vested in the employer 24 contributions made under AS 39.35.750(a). A disabled member is fully vested in the 25 contributions to the member's individual account made under this subsection. An 26 employee is not entitled to elect distributions from the employee's individual 27 contribution account under AS 39.35.810 while the employee is receiving disability 28 benefits under this section. While an employee is receiving disability benefits, based 29 on the disabled employee's gross monthly compensation at the time of termination due 30 to disability, the employer shall make contributions 31 (1) to the employee's individual account under AS 39.35.730 on behalf 01 of the employee, without deduction from the employee's disability payments; and 02 (2) on behalf of the employee under AS 39.35.750. 03 * Sec. 6. This Act takes effect immediately under AS 01.10.070(c).

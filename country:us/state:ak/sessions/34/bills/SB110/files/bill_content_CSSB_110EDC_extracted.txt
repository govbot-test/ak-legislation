AS 14.57 is amended by adding a new section to read: 05 Article 4. Museum Construction, Expansion, and Major Renovation Grants. 06 Sec. 14.57.300. Museum construction, expansion, and major renovation 07 matching grant program. (a) A matching grant program for museum construction, 08 expansion, and major renovation is established in the Department of Education and 09 Early Development. A grant may be awarded to a museum 10 (1) for construction of a new facility; 11 (2) to increase the square footage of an existing facility by at least 10 12 percent; 13 (3) to physically upgrade an existing facility in a way that allows the 14 museum to better preserve and present artifacts to the public; or 01 (4) to improve a space in an existing facility to allow a museum to 02 improve the educational services provided to the public. 03 (b) A person in charge of construction, expansion, or renovation of an eligible 04 museum may apply to the department for a matching grant under regulations adopted 05 by the department. 06 (c) To be eligible for a grant under this section, an applicant must 07 (1) apply on a form provided by the department; 08 (2) be located in the state; and 09 (3) provide matching funds from other sources in an amount equal to at 10 least 50 percent of the total proposed grant project costs. 11 (d) Subject to appropriation, the department shall award not more than 50 12 percent of the total proposed grant project costs to an eligible applicant. 13 (e) The department shall establish regulations to implement this section. 14 (f) In this section, "museum" has the meaning given in AS 14.57.290. 15 * Sec. 2. This Act takes effect immediately under AS 01.10.070(c).

HCR 301: Suspending Rules 47 and 55, Uniform Rules of the Alaska State Legislature, relating to the carry-over of bills to a special session. 00 HOUSE CONCURRENT RESOLUTION NO. 301 01 Suspending Rules 47 and 55, Uniform Rules of the Alaska State Legislature, relating to 02 the carry-over of bills to a special session. 03 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF ALASKA: 04 That under Rule 54, Uniform Rules of the Alaska State Legislature, the provisions of 05 Rule 47 of the Uniform Rules regarding the carry-over of bills and the provisions of Rule 55 06 of the Uniform Rules regarding the use of Mason's Manual of Legislative Procedure, 2020 07 edition, to cover situations not directly addressed by the Uniform Rules are suspended to the 08 extent they prohibit the carry-over of bills and resolutions from the Second Regular Session 09 and the Second Special Session to the Third Special Session of the Thirty-Fourth Alaska State 10 Legislature; and be it 11 FURTHER RESOLVED that this resolution applies only to the following bill 12 previously under consideration during the Second Regular Session and the Second Special 13 Session of the Thirty-Fourth Alaska State Legislature: 14 House Bill 381: An Act relating to the taxation of certain natural gas pipeline 15 property; relating to municipal taxation limitations; establishing an alternative 16 volumetric tax on natural gas throughput; relating to the allocation of revenue from the 01 alternative volumetric tax; and providing for an effective date; and be it 02 FURTHER RESOLVED that this resolution is retroactive to June 20, 2026, at 10:00 03 a.m., the beginning of the Third Special Session of the Thirty-Fourth Alaska State Legislature.
